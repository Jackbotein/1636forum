Our Mission

The 1636 Leadership Forum was founded by six Harvard students seeking to bring thought leaders in their respective realms of interest to Harvard to share their insights with students. Speakers span government, academia, business, and philanthrophy - which mirrors the multitude of careers student attendees dream of pursuing. Events are open to Harvard College students who present valid HUID, and Harvard affiliate and other individuals upon invitation. All 1636 Leadership Forum Events are held off the record, and filming events is prohibited.

CPhillip Eng is the General Manager and CEO of the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority (MBTA).
